A Lifetime of Discarded Purchases

UNFASHIONED

This project is a data visualisation of the hidden lifecycle of our clothes. Built on garment consumption estimates of an average fast fashion shopper, it maps a lifetime of discarded items — from t-shirts to sneakers — into particle-based forms. Rather than abstract statistics, it offers a spatial, bodily perspective on textile waste, inviting viewers to see accumulation as something personal, not distant.
